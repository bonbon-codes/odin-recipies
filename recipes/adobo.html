Yummy Slow Cooked Adobo

Description

Adobo is a quintessential Filipino dish, essentially a flavorful stew where meat (like chicken, pork, or beef), seafood, or vegetables are marinated and braised in a mixture of vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, black peppercorns, and bay leaves.

Ingredients

  • 1 small sweet onion, sliced
  • ¾ cup low sodium soy sauce
  • 8 cloves garlic, crushed
  • ½ cup vinegar
  • 1 (3 pound) whole chicken, cut into pieces
