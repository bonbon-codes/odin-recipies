Yummy Kare Kare

Description

AKare Kare is a traditional Filipino stew complimented with a thick savory peanut sauce. The commonly used meats for this dish are ox tail, tripe, and pork leg; on some occasions goat and chicken meat are also used.

Ingredients

  • Oxtails
  • Onion, garlic:
  • Beef bouillon cube
  • Peanut butter (unsweetened)
  • Bagoong
